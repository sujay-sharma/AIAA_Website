Why get involved in AIAA ?

Our goal as an organization has always been to inspire and motivate engineers to pursue aerospace opportunity by creating a platform that ensures that members are able to engage in unique collegiate-level experiences. A partnership with AIAA UTD would ensure that you have firsthand access to the most gifted and passionate engineers, create a pathway for you to increase your visibility on campus, and give your organization an opportunity to directly promote aspiring aerospace engineers and their pursuits in the industry.
